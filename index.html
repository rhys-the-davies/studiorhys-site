Amsterdam, 2026

I've spent my career scaling impact through product. Built global communities at Ubuntu. Launched climate software at South Pole. Rebuilt waste-data infrastructure at Earth Action.

Mission-driven organisations rarely struggle for ideas. They struggle to turn ideas into shipped, measurable work. That's the gap I fill.

I work independently, with one team at a time. Twelve to twenty-four week engagements. Embedded enough to make decisions; detached enough to ask hard questions.
